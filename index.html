About Us

YOUTHPRINUA is a youth led organization of innovative and talented members joining together through youth empowerment, environmental conservation, art and talent, sanitation, health and hygiene. It was founded in the year 2020. These youths are mainly raised and brought up in the informal settlements of Nairobi, Kenya. These areas are marred with drastic challenges i.e. poverty, drug abuse, prostitution, criminal activities, pollution and poor waste management. Against all odds, we have stood steadfast in promoting positive change through capacity building, equipping the community members with knowledge and skills to be the drivers of positive change in order to raise their livelihoods and wellbeing.

YOUTHPRINUA is a combination of three words “Youth”, “entrepreneurship” and “Inua” which is a Swahili word meaning to develop or uplift. As a group we are not only improving ourselves but also the entire community members, especially the youths at large through capacity building, entrepreneurship and inculcating a culture of creativity thus raising their living standards.

Our Programs

TECH IN ALL

TECH IN ALL project aims to bring the community together through digital transformation and ICT.

YOUTH EMPOWERMENT

YOUTHPRINUA opens a gateway to intergenerational equity, assets enhancement, civic engagement and democracy building and all programs that focus on positive youth development

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSERVATION

YOUTHPRINUA also play important role in environmental conservation and awareness. These include environmental education, climate action, waste management, sanitation and hygiene. We sensitize the community members on efficient and sustainable action towards nature.

ARTS, SPORTS AND TALENT

YOUTHPRINUA plays a pivotal role in encouraging youths to use their god gifted talents through arts and sports to amplify their voices towards positivity in the community. We have a sports academy that aims to train, motivate, mentor and encourage the young in exploiting their talents to full potential.
